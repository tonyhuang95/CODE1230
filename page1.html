ABOUT SYDNEY

What is the data set?

Age by sex of individuals living in NSW

Why is the data set collected?

To show the distribution of individuals by age within NSW

Which government departments would use this?

The departments that would use this data are:
Department of Education and Training - uses this data to ensure schooling is distributed appropriately around NSW.
Department of Employment - uses this data to ensure that Australians find and keep employment.
Department of Human Services - uses this data to see the distribution of youth and adults.


Which private sector firm would use this?

Aged care firms would use this data to place their investments in the optimal areas.

How would this data set change the way you think about space?

This data shows how age distributes around NSW, thus following the trend, it correlates to the cost of housing where young adults are unable to afford housing in the suburbs and would rent apartments in the city to be closer to work.
